# Privacy Policy for Material digital clock widget with google fonts

**Effective date:** [2026-04-07]

This Privacy Policy explains how **Yoav Z** ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the app **Material digital clock widget with google fonts** ("the App").

## 1. Information we collect

The App is designed to be lightweight and privacy-friendly.

We do **not** collect, store, sell, or share any personal information from users.

The App does **not**:
- require account registration
- collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data
- use analytics
- use advertising SDKs
- track users across apps or websites
- sell user data

## 2. How the App works

The App is used only to display a digital clock widget and related visual styling features.

Any settings you choose in the App are stored locally on your device and are not sent to us.

## 3. Third-party services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If the App loads font resources or other visual assets, those resources are used only to provide the App’s appearance and are not used to identify you personally.

## 4. Data storage

The App does not create a user account and does not maintain a cloud profile for you.

Any app preferences are stored locally on your device.

## 5. Data sharing

We do not share user data with third parties because we do not collect user data in the first place.

## 6. Data retention and deletion

Because the App does not collect personal data, there is no user data retained by us on our servers.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored app settings are removed from your device according to Android’s normal app behavior.

## 7. Children’s privacy

The App is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Since the App does not collect personal data from any users, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 8. Security

We take reasonable measures to keep the App simple and minimize data exposure by avoiding personal-data collection altogether.

## 9. Open source

The App is open source. Public source code availability does not change this Privacy Policy.
